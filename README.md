MoonLight Energy Solutions aims to enhance operational efficiency and sustainability through strategic solar investments. This project delivers a complete analytical framework for evaluating solar potential across Benin, Sierra Leone, and Togo.
Business Objectives
✅ Identify high-potential regions for solar installation
✅ Assess resource stability and operational risk
✅ Provide data-driven recommendations aligned with sustainability goals
✅ Support strategic decision-making with statistical evidence
✅ Optimize long-term investment portfolio strategy
